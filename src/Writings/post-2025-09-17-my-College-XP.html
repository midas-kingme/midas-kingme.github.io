My College Experience in a Nutshell

Published: September 17, 2025

College has honestly sucked from the moment I got here. Granted, I could have been more prepared, mentally, financially, etc..

They'll tell you there scholarships, there's financial aid, there's ways to get by. That's bullshit! lol

So I graduated from high school back in Summer 2013. Sylacauga High School in Sylacauga, Alabama. Damn that's wild. Anywho, I immediately started classes at Central Alabama Community College that following summer. I was granted a show choir scholarship where as long as I participated in several of the hosted showcases, I was given a full ride to take classes.

As ashamed as I am to admit, I was never that into sports. I mean I played baseball a good bit throughout youth. I was heavily into theatre, and reading, and computers. I was what you would call a "Disney kid". Fuckin embarrassing lol. So, I'm just now starting out as a freshman and was basically given the hookup. You mean to tell me every single one of my courses are paid for, I still get a grant from FAFSA in case I need textbooks, and all I gotta do is attend class and sing? Shiiid..Why not?

I feel so fuckin beta writing this.

Fuggit. I'm about to get my Zac Efron on. I had participated in some shows early on in teenagehood so I had little to no nervous jitters. Plus I saw it as an opportunity to act a fool in front of the crowd.
